HOW IT WORKS

Sign Up

For you to start using our system, you have to sign up for your school, and get school admin portal.

Step One

Add Edmin/Editor(s)

Login to your school admin dashboard and add the school ICT admin or teacher who will be in charge of the admin section of the school management system.

Step Two

Register Student(s)

The school admin Login to your school admin dashboard and registers/Add students, copy each of their reg numbers and and distribute to the students. The registration number is a one time number asigned to each of the students which serves as his/her identity.

Step Three

Download the resultsheets

Download the resultsheets for each of the subjects and distribute to the teachers according to the subject they handle. Step Four

Publish Result

When the editors(teachers) are through with the result computation, they can now publish the result online using the editor account information you setup for them in step two.

After they have uploaded the result, the school admin will have to change the result status to published. This will make it posible for students to check that particular result

Note: The previllage to change the result status it exclusively reserved for the school admin only and can't be executed by the editor..

Step Five

Give students pin

After successfully uploading all the results for each of the classes, then go ahead to distribute the result checking scratch cards to each of the students.  through our reseller or direct from us, it will  be delivered to you as soon as possible.
Note: A scratch card can only be used by a student per term and can only be used to check the same result for maximum of 5 times only
